Floormate is a mobile app helps student avoid awkward confrontation when addressing their noise complaints, while also helping Residential Advisors facilitate positive conflict resolution.


Categories :UI/UX Design, Graphic Design, Web Development, Branding

Released :10 Mar 2015

In my Design Thinking and Doing Course, we were given the project, Improve Northwestern. The goal was to address an issue relevant to life and culture at Northwestern which we felt represents a substantive design problem. My two teammates and I found a design opportunity in the issue of noise in college dorms.
