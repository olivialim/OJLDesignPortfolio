About Olivia

With an Art History background at Northwestern University, I was inspired to seek for an avenue for creativity and problem solving. Inspired to find this space, four friends and I founded a multimedia publication that opened the door to front-end development and design. This inspired me to take a quarter to learn about iOS Development at the Flatiron School in New York City. I took this knowledge into my collaborative courses at Northwestern and have found ways to combine my design and iOS development skills.
